Blackjack

Cheat Sheet

Player Actions

Hit [ F]
Take another card from dealer
Stand [ Space]
Take no more cards. Ends player turn.
Split [ A]
Option that is available if both cards dealt to player are of same value. They will be split into two hands which they player plays both separately. On this blackjack tutorial, split should be chosen if strategy mandates it, but the hand is not split and the player's next action should be treated as if split is not available. Only available first turn.
Double Down [ S]
Increase bet by 100% followed by taking one hit and standing. Only available first turn.
Surrender [ D]
House takes half of player's bet. Player automatically loses. Only available first turn.
Insurance [ G]
Available if dealer's face-up card is an ace. This is a side bet that the dealer has blackjack. Only available first turn.

General Gameplay

The player receives two cards face-up and the dealer receives one card face-up and another card face-down. The object of the game is for the player to reach 21 or closer to 21 than the dealer without going over 21. Face-cards are counted as 10. Aces count as either 1 or 11. A hand with the ace counting as 11 is known as a soft hand while a hand with the ace counting as 1 is called a hard hand. If the player's count is over 21 or the dealer gets closer to 21 than the player without going over 21, the player loses. After the players stands, the dealer will hit until the dealer has a total of 17 or more. A blackjack occurs when a player has one 10 value card and one ace. The player will automatically win a hand with blackjack unless the dealer also has it, which results in a push, or a tie.
